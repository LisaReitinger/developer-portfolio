Hi, I'm Lisa

I’m a front-end developer with a background in writing and content. My interest in tech started while working at Powertrain Ventures in Mexico — I was really drawn to what the development team was doing and how they brought ideas to life through code.

It took me some time to make the switch, but once I started learning at the Code Institute, everything clicked. I’ve been building mobile-first, responsive projects with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I’m now learning React to take things further.

I enjoy how front-end development combines structure with creativity — the process of solving problems, designing clean interfaces, and constantly learning something new keeps me motivated. I'm still growing, but I’m focused, curious, and genuinely excited about where this path is taking me.

Also, I believe good problem-solving starts with a calm mind... and maybe a cup of coffee (or two).
